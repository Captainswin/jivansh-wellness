Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024  |  Jivansh Wellness, Ranchi, Jharkhand

Jivansh Wellness is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or place an order with us.

1. Information We Collect

When you register or place an order on our website, we may collect personally identifiable information including your name, email address, mobile number, delivery address, pincode, and browsing activity on our site. We may also receive updated delivery information from our logistics partners.

You may choose not to share your personal information with us, but this may limit your ability to use some features of our website or complete a purchase.

2. Why We Collect Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders and communicate order status
  • To verify your identity and enable secure transactions
  • To improve our website performance and user experience
  • To share relevant product recommendations and promotional offers
  • To respond to your queries and provide customer support
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information when you interact with our website — through account registration, placing orders, browsing products, or contacting our support team. Our servers may use cookies or similar technologies to improve your experience.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for commercial gain. We may share information with:

  • Logistics partners to fulfil and deliver your orders
  • Payment gateway partners to securely process transactions
  • Regulatory or legal authorities when required by law

All third-party partners are bound by appropriate data agreements and cannot use your information beyond what is necessary.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and by using our website, you acknowledge this inherent limitation.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for adults. Minors under the age of 18 may browse our website but are not permitted to register or make purchases without adult supervision or consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We review and update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page and take effect immediately upon posting. We encourage you to check this page regularly.
