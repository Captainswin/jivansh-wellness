Cancellation & Return Policy

Last updated: January 2024  |  Jivansh Wellness

At Jivansh Wellness, we want every customer to be satisfied with their purchase. This policy outlines how you can cancel an order or initiate a return or refund.

Order Cancellation

You may cancel your order before it has been dispatched from our warehouse. To cancel, contact our support team via email or phone with your order details. Once an order has been dispatched, it cannot be cancelled — however, you may initiate a return after delivery.

For pre-dispatch cancellations, the full amount will be refunded to your original payment method within 2 to 5 business days.

Returns & Replacements

We accept return or replacement requests within 5 days of delivery. To be eligible for a return, the product must be:

  • In its original, unopened packaging with seals and labels intact
  • Accompanied by the original invoice
  • Not a product that was provided as a free gift or sample

How to Raise a Return Request

Step 1: Email us at info@jivanshwellness.com within 5 days of receiving your order with your order ID and reason for return.

Step 2: Attach clear photos or a short video showing the product and its condition, including the batch details visible on the packaging.

Step 3: Our team will review your request within 3 business days. If approved, we will arrange a reverse pickup and process your refund or replacement.

Damaged or Wrong Products

If you receive a product that is damaged, leaking, or incorrect, please contact us within 5 days of delivery with supporting images or video clearly showing the issue and the batch number on the product. We will arrange a replacement or issue a full refund based on your preference.

Returns Not Accepted When

  • The return request is raised after 5 days of delivery
  • The product has been opened or the seal tampered with
  • Original packaging, labels, or barcodes are missing or damaged
  • The product is a complimentary item or free gift

Refund Process

Once your return is received and verified at our warehouse, refunds are processed within 2 to 5 business days. The refund will be credited to the original payment method. Please allow an additional 2 to 3 days for the amount to reflect in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.
