Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2024  |  Jivansh Wellness

At Jivansh Wellness, we are committed to delivering your orders promptly and in perfect condition. We partner with reliable logistics providers across India to ensure a smooth delivery experience.

Shipping Charges

Orders above ₹499 qualify for free shipping. A shipping charge of ₹99 applies to orders below ₹499. The applicable charge will be clearly shown at checkout before you confirm your order.

Order Processing

Once your order is placed and payment is confirmed, our team processes it within 24 hours on business days. Each product is inspected for quality before being packed and dispatched. You will receive a confirmation message with your tracking details once your order has been shipped.

Delivery Timeframe

Estimated delivery time is 3 to 7 business days from the date of dispatch, depending on your location. Metro cities typically receive orders within 3 to 4 days, while deliveries to remote or rural areas may take up to 7 days. We strive to meet these timelines but cannot guarantee delivery dates in cases of unforeseen delays by our logistics partners.

Packaging

All products are carefully packed with appropriate protective material to prevent damage during transit. Fragile or liquid items such as syrups are given an extra layer of protection. Jivansh Wellness takes responsibility for any damage that occurs during shipping.

Delivery Coverage

We ship to almost all pin codes across India. You can check serviceability for your pin code on our product pages. If your area is not currently serviceable, please reach out to us and we will do our best to assist you.

Order Tracking

Once dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via SMS or email. You can use this to check your shipment status with our logistics partner at any time.

Delivery Attempts

Our delivery partner will attempt delivery up to 3 times. If all attempts are unsuccessful, the package will be returned to our warehouse. Please ensure someone is available at the delivery address during business hours to receive the package.
